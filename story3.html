Incident 3: The Triwizard Tournament

The Triwizard Tournament brought challenges beyond imagination, testing courage, wit, and survival. Fire blazed in arenas, merpeople lurked beneath icy waters, and dragons guarded treasures with eyes that pierced the soul. Harry, alone and hunted by fate, faced each trial knowing that one misstep could mean death. Shadows of competitors flickered like phantoms, danger threaded every breath, and the air trembled with spells and curses. Magic was a weapon and a shield, courage a lifeline, and fear the silent companion that never left his side. Every moment demanded perfection, every heartbeat echoed peril, and the haunting beauty of survival painted a tragic portrait of a hero forged in flames of despair.
